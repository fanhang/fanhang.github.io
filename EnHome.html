About me

I received his B.E. degree from School of Electrical Information (Wu Yuzhang College), Sichuan University in 2015 and Ph.D. degree from Department of Electrical Engineering and Applied Electronics Technology, Tsinghua University in 2021, during which he minored in Tsinghua University Data Science Competency Enhancement Program. In August 2017, he visited the School of Engineering and Applied Science, Harvard University, USA. He is now a postdoctoral fellow at the Wudaokou School of Finance, Tsinghua University (jointly trained with the Institute of Cross-Information, Tsinghua University), where his main research interests include privacy computing and data transaction pricing, fintech and energy economics. He has participated in several digital economy, financial technology and smart grid and smart high speed rail big data projects (including the digital identity authentication and personal data account project chaired by Wu Xiaoling, former vice governor of the central bank, and the key project of the Natural Science Foundation in the digital economy category chaired by Professor Huang Lei of the Department of Information Management, School of Economics and Management, Beijing Jiaotong University and Ms. Deng Xiaomei of the Department of Construction and Management, Tsinghua University). He has published 14 SCI/EI papers and participated in writing one industry standard in privacy computing. He has also won the National Scholarship, Tang Lixin Scholarship and Tsinghua Data Science Institute RONG Scholarship Grand Prize. He has also received the National Scholarship, Tang Lixin Scholarship and Tsinghua Data Science Institute RONG Scholarship. He has interned at financial institutions such as Harvest Fund and Shenwan Hongyuan Securities, passed the securities qualification exam, and is currently preparing for the CFA exam.

Research Interests: Privacy Computing, Data Trading, Machine Learning, Fintech, Energy Economics.
